Nearby Friends

During Fall 2015, I was a product design intern at Facebook where I worked on Nearby Friends, an opt-in real-time location product.

I learned a lot from working in a product space which encompassed challenges like approximate location, reciprocal sharing, and designing for scale. I was the second designer on the Real-Time Location Team and worked with an extensive crossfunctional team.

Most my work is pre-launch, but keep an eye out!
