Introduction to Illustrator & Photoshop

My sophomore year, I facilitated a 2-unit course called Introduction to Illustrator & Photoshop under Berkeleyâ€™s Democratic Education (DeCal) program. Both semesters, I taught technical design skills to a weekly 2-hour class of 30 students. I also planned lessons, held office hours, graded homework, and led our Education Team.

The class is widely popular. That being said, we try to keep class-sizes small, make the experience personal, and provide a high-quality educational experience. Our class has been taught by students for students every semester since Fall 2010. We've been around for a while, and we're not going anywhere.

When I taught, we used a third-party educational hosting platform where we posted lessons and files. The platform was great for grading but required us to repost all class information and materials every semester. It was often difficult to navigate, buggy in its upload process, and a headache to style.

After two semesters of using it, I pushed our Education Team to make a switch. I designed and developed an independent site where we can consistently host lesson overviews. This way, when we start anew each semester, the online setup requires minimal effort. The site also gives prospective students an overview of the course before they even apply.

And apply too if you're a Berkeley student interested!

A PERSONAL NOTE

I love this class with all my heart. I took this DeCal my first semester at Berkeley. It was my first exposure to any sort of design, and it gave me the technical skills to get me on my feet. When I applied for a leadership position, I knew I wanted to teach so I could help others make the transition I did.

This semester, I've chosen to step away from teaching in order to dedicate more time to other pursuits. I realized I learned a tremendous amount by leading my own class, and I wanted to open up the opportunity for others to do the same. I'm excited for two good friends of mine who were my TA's and are now both going to teach! Best wishes to both of them, as I'm sure they'll do an amazing job. As for me, I'll be advising our Education Team and managing the website.
